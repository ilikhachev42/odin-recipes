Chebureki

Description

Chebureki are deep-fried turnovers with a filling of ground or minced meat and onions. They are made with a single round piece of dough folded over the filling in a crescent shape.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of flour
  • 250ml of water
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 pinches of salt
  • 1kg of ground meat
  • 2 onions

Order

  1. Mix flour, water, mayonnaise and salt.
  2. Cut onions and mix it with ground meat.
  3. Make a form like on a picture.
